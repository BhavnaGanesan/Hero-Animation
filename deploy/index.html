Philadelphia Ceramics

Our Mission

Philadelphia Ceramics inspires curiosity and discovery around the art and craft of clay, drawing together students, artists, and an engaged public into a welcoming community. Every day, in our classrooms, studios, galleries, and neighborhoods, we deepen the connection between people and clay with the highest quality programs and exhibitions. Founded in 1974, Philadelphia Ceramics continues in the belief that shared creativity, so fundamental to humanity, is a critical force for good.
